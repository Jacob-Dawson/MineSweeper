How To Play:

  1. POV: You are in the bomb squad and are trying to diffuse bombs but you dont know where they are.

  2. Click on any of the tiles to start playing. Clicking on them will uncover either a bomb, a blank tile or a tile with a number.

  3. Tiles with numbers indicate that the squares next them have bombs. For example a "2" tile means that there are 2 tiles that contain bombs next to the tiles.

  4. If you think that there is a bomb under a tile you can right click / long press a tile to put a flag ontop of it, that way you wont accidently click on it. You have a limited amount of flags.

  5. You can also click on already revealed tiles (with numbers) if you have appropiate amount of flags next to them to reveal all the tiles around them.

  6. If you want to restart the level or play a new one, then press the emoji / smiley to reset it.
